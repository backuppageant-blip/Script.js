— About The House

The House Behind
The Crown

M&A Pageantry is a Delta State-based pageantry, modeling, and brand representation house, built to turn public votes and honest competition into real opportunity.

We built M&A Pageantry to give contestants across Nigeria a transparent, verifiable stage: a live vote count, a secure Paystack-processed voting pipeline, and a public leaderboard that updates in real time — not a closed-door panel deciding a winner behind the scenes.

Today the house runs three divisions under one roof — the monthly Face of M&A Pageantry vote, a working Modeling Sector registry, and a Brand Ambassador placement track — alongside a vendor Marketplace and a ticketed live annual event, all based in Delta State, Nigeria.

Transparency

Every vote is verified server-side before it counts — no manual tallying, no disputed numbers.

Access

Pageantry registration is free. We charge for votes, not for the right to compete.

Opportunity

Real cash prizes, ambassador placements, and modeling contracts — not just a sash and a photo.

Community

A live Chatbox, WhatsApp line, and social channels keep contestants and voters in constant touch with the house.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about voting, registering, and getting paid out across all three divisions.

Voting

How much does a vote cost?

Each vote costs ₦250, charged securely through Paystack. The current price is always shown on the Face of M&A Pageantry page before you confirm payment.

When does voting open and close?

Voting runs monthly, opening on the 1st and closing on the 21st. Once the window closes, votes for that cycle are final and the leaderboard locks.

Can I vote more than once?

Yes. There's no cap on votes per person — you can cast as many paid votes for your favourite contestant as you like while the window is open.

Are votes refundable?

No. Because votes count immediately toward the live leaderboard, all verified vote payments are final and non-refundable.

Registration & Fees

Is it free to enter the pageant?

Yes — registering as a contestant in the Face of M&A Pageantry division is completely free.

What does it cost to register elsewhere?

Modeling Sector registration is ₦5,000, Brand Ambassador registration is ₦15,000, and Marketplace brand registration is ₦20,000. All are one-time fees.

Is the registration fee refundable if I'm not accepted?

Registration fees cover application processing and review, so they're non-refundable regardless of outcome. Please review each division's requirements before applying.

Payments & Verification

How are payments processed?

All payments — votes, registrations, tickets, and Chatbox entry — run through Paystack, then are verified server-side before anything is credited to your account or the leaderboard.

I paid but it hasn't shown up. What do I do?

Verification is usually instant. If a payment doesn't reflect within a few minutes, message our WhatsApp support line with your payment reference and email, and our team will check it manually.

How do prize winners get paid?

Top-ranked contestants at the end of each voting cycle are contacted directly using the phone number or email on their registration to arrange cash prize payout.

Support

How can I reach the M&A Pageantry team?

The fastest way is WhatsApp. You can also use the live Chatbox, email us, or reach out on Instagram, TikTok, X, or Facebook — all listed on our Contact page.

Where can I read the full rules?

See our Terms & Conditions page for the complete rules covering eligibility, voting, prizes, and conduct.
